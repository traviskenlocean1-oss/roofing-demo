FAQ

Common
Questions

How long does a full roof replacement take?

Most residential roofs are completed in a single day. Larger homes or commercial properties may take 2-3 days. We provide a detailed schedule at your free inspection so you know exactly what to expect.

Do you work with insurance companies?

Yes — we specialize in insurance claims. Our team documents all damage, communicates directly with your adjuster, and handles the paperwork so you receive the maximum payout you're entitled to. We work with all major carriers.

What warranty do you offer on your work?

All installations include full manufacturer material warranties (up to 50 years on premium shingles) plus our own lifetime workmanship warranty. If anything fails due to installation, we come back and fix it at no charge.

Do you offer emergency roofing services?

Absolutely. Our emergency line is available 24/7, 365 days a year. Storm damage, sudden leaks, or structural failures — we dispatch crews within hours. Temporary tarping and emergency repairs are available around the clock.

How do I know if I need a repair or full replacement?

Our free inspection gives you an honest assessment — we only recommend replacement when it's genuinely the better long-term value. If a repair will solve the problem for years to come, we'll tell you. We won't upsell work you don't need.

What roofing materials do you work with?

We install all major roofing systems: architectural and 3-tab asphalt shingles, standing seam metal, tile and slate, flat roof TPO and EPDM, modified bitumen, and wood shake. We only use premium-grade materials from certified manufacturers.

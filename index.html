What We Do

COMPLETE
ROOFING
SOLUTIONS

From storm repairs to full replacements, Summit Roofing Co handles every job with precision, speed, and lasting quality.

01

ROOF INSTALLATION

New construction and full replacement. We work with asphalt shingles, metal roofing, tile, and flat systems — all backed by manufacturer warranties.

02

STORM REPAIR

Rapid response after hail, wind, or hurricane damage. We assess, document, and repair fast — and work directly with your insurance company.

03

ROOF INSPECTION

Comprehensive visual and moisture inspections. Catch small problems before they become expensive disasters. Annual plans available.

04

GUTTERS & FLASHING

Proper gutter installation and flashing prevents water intrusion. We seal every edge, valley, and penetration point to keep your home dry.

05

COMMERCIAL ROOFING

TPO, EPDM, and modified bitumen systems for commercial properties. We minimize downtime and meet code on every project.

06

MAINTENANCE PLANS

Seasonal maintenance keeps your roof performing for decades. Our plans include bi-annual inspections, cleaning, and priority repair access.

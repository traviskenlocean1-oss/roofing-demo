FAQ

Common
Questions

How long does a full roof replacement take?

Most residential roofs are completed in a single day. Larger homes or commercial properties may take 2–3 days. We provide a detailed schedule at your free inspection.

Do you work with insurance companies?

Yes — we specialize in insurance claims. Our team documents all damage, communicates directly with your adjuster, and handles paperwork so you receive the maximum payout you're entitled to.

What warranty do you offer on your work?

All installations include full manufacturer material warranties — up to 50 years on premium shingles — plus our own lifetime workmanship warranty. If anything fails due to installation, we fix it at no charge.

Do you offer emergency roofing services?

Our emergency line is available 24/7, 365 days a year. Storm damage, sudden leaks, or structural failures — we dispatch crews within hours. Temporary tarping available around the clock.

How do I know if I need a repair or replacement?

Our free inspection gives you an honest assessment. We only recommend replacement when it's genuinely the better long-term value. We won't upsell work you don't need.

What roofing materials do you work with?

We install architectural shingles, standing seam metal, tile and slate, flat roof TPO and EPDM, modified bitumen, and wood shake. Premium-grade materials from certified manufacturers only.
